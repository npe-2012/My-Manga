MY MANGA

BY ME FOR YOU

MY MANGA is a trifle I wrote one night to hold some manga translations I was doing, or planning on doing.

I wanted a simple and beautiful interface but one that would let readers concentrate on the manga page. I picked a few open fonts, three colours, and the new tools of the web, and hop! that was it. I hope you like it...

Nevermind that the title says BY ME FOR YOU, none of the manga hosted here are actually mine. Sure, I translated some of them, but that is nothing compared to the true genius of the authors.

Oh, and are you worried that I'm robbing the mangaka of their copyright? Be reassured: not here. Either I've got permission, or the work is free...
